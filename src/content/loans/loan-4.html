How to Get a Business Loan in 2024

Step 1: Know Your Numbers

  • Credit score (Personal: 680+, Business: 80+ on FICO SBSS)
  • Time in business (6+ months for most online lenders, 2+ years for banks)
  • Revenue ($50K+/year for many lenders)
  • Debt-to-income ratio (<50% ideal)

Step 2: Choose the Right Lender

Lender Type Speed Approval Odds
Banks Slow (2-6 weeks) Hard (Strict requirements)
Credit Unions Medium Medium (Member-focused)
Online Lenders Fast (24h-7d) Easier (Higher rates)
Microlenders Varies High (For underserved businesses)

Step 3: Prepare Your Documents

  • Business plan (For startups/new expansions)
  • 6-12 months of bank statements
  • Tax returns (Personal + business)
  • Profit & loss statements
  • Legal documents (Licenses, contracts, leases)
"A disorganized loan application is like showing up to a job interview in pajamas."

Step 4: Apply Strategically

  • Apply to 3-5 lenders to compare rates
  • Avoid multiple hard credit checks (Space applications within 14 days)
  • Negotiate terms (Fees, prepayment penalties)
